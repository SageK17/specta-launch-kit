05 — For the team · how it's built

Extend it in minutes

Make a new ad

Every ad is a row of data. Add an entry to ads/build.js (image, format, theme, headline), then:

cd assets/inspo-2/ads
node build.js          # writes HTML + manifest + gallery
node render-still.js   # renders every ad to a 2× PNG

Formats: 45 (1080×1350), 916 (1080×1920), 11 (1080×1080). Headlines support | line-breaks and {coral}…{/coral}.

Make a new scene

Scenes are generated from real product cut-outs with a locked prompt so the logo and shape stay pixel-accurate. The recipes + the exact prompt are in the Visual Guide. New scene images drop into series/ and become available to build.js.

Quality rule: always seed from the real photos-real cut-outs + product shots, and keep the logo-lock paragraph — never let a generator redraw the SPECTA mark.
