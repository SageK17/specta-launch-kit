How these actually go viral
  • Hook in the first 1 second — text on screen + motion before the brain scrolls.
  • Use a trending sound — add it in CapCut; native audio > any music we bake in.
  • Look organic — phone-shot, real hands, real desks. Polished = skipped.
  • Post 4–7×/week — volume + variety; let the algorithm find the winner, then double down.
  • Reply to every comment with a video — free reach, and "is it worth it?" is a built-in script.
  • One idea per video — the pop is the star. CTA soft: "link in bio", not "BUY NOW".
